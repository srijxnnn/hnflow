# Privacy Policy for HNFlow

**Last updated:** 9 January 2026

HNFlow is a third-party, open-source Hacker News client built using React Native and Expo. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the app works.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

HNFlow does **not collect, store, or share** any personal information.  
No user accounts, emails, or identifiers are required to use the app.

---

## 2. Data from Hacker News

HNFlow fetches public content from the **Hacker News Algolia API**, including:

- Stories
- Comments
- Usernames
- Points and timestamps

This data is publicly available and not associated with your identity.

---

## 3. Analytics & Tracking

HNFlow does **not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs**.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The app uses:

- Hacker News Algolia API
- Expo platform services (only for app functionality)

No personal data is sent to these services.
